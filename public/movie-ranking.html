The Best Movies According to Onomono

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Wall-e

A beautiful movie about how a future where there's no life in the planet and hummans live in aircrafts just sitting and eating. Meanwhile, Wall-e, a trash robot who lives on Earth, finds a proof of life and meet Eva whom he fall in love. It's about friendship, love and make reflect on how we're treating our planet.

Spirited Away

One of my favourite movies from Hayao Miyazaki. It's an allegory about the horrors of capitalism and society's toxic obsession with work. It's also about bravery, love and friendship.

Lion King

This is a classic. Lion prince Simba and his father are targeted by his bitter uncle Scar, who wants to ascend the throne himself. It's about love, loss, facing our fears, forgiveness, friendship and growing.
